Certified Developer

After having my first daughter, I knew the pizzeria was not going to allow me to provide what I wanted for my new found purpose in life. Two years of hard labor in construction, ending in a layoff, I found myself with nothing to show for it. I knew at this point I had to return to higher education. I wanted to earn something that could never be taken away from me. I always was computer literate but did not find my love for analytical thinking until I took a Programming Logic class. Never did I know that the languages that control computers could be so interesting. Here I am doing what I love and will keep growing as a developer.
